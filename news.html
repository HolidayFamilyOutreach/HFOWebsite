Toy Day Event - Saturday, December 9, 2023 – St James Lutheran Church – by invitation

posted Nov 21, 2022 By Julie Keyser

For 70 years, Holiday Family Outreach has been delivering Christmas joy to thousands of low-income families in Adams County. The annual Toy Day Event provides toys for children of low income families, ages 0-12 years old. Donations are down this year, but the need is up. Will you help us meet the need by donating here?

Adams County Community Foundation Giving Spree! Thursday, November 9, 2023

posted Sep 23, 2022 By Julie Keyser

Drop off Your HFO Donation:

Bring your completed Giving Spree Donation Form and gift to the Gettysburg Middle School on November 9 between 3:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. You’ll get a personal thank-you from our Holiday Family Outreach Board Members!

Mail in Your HFO Donation:

Complete the Giving Spree Donation Form for Holiday Family Outreach and mail it with your gift to: Adams County Community Foundation, 25 South 4th St. Gettysburg, PA 17325. Please mail your donation early to ensure we have your gift in time for the Giving Spree on November 9th.

Donate Online via the ACCF Website:

Visit the Adams County Community Foundation website on November 9th any time between 12:01 a.m. and 12:00 midnight to complete your HFO donation online. It’s quick, convenient and secure!
